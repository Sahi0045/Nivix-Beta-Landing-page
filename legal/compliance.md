# Compliance

**Last Updated:** January 7, 2025

## 1. Regulatory Compliance Framework

NIVIXPE PRIVATE LIMITED is committed to maintaining the highest standards of legal and regulatory compliance. Our operations adhere to the following Indian laws and regulations:

### 1.1 Prevention of Money Laundering Act (PMLA), 2002
- Implementation of KYC (Know Your Customer) procedures
- Monitoring and reporting of suspicious transactions
- Maintenance of transaction records for 5 years
- Designated Compliance Officer

### 1.2 Foreign Exchange Management Act (FEMA), 1999
- Compliance with Liberalized Remittance Scheme (LRS) limits
- Proper documentation for cross-border transactions
- Adherence to RBI guidelines on foreign exchange

### 1.3 Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA), 2023
- Data protection and privacy measures
- User consent management
- Data subject rights implementation
- Data breach notification procedures

### 1.4 Income Tax Act, 1961
- TDS compliance (Section 194S)
- Income reporting and documentation
- Maintenance of financial records

### 1.5 Goods and Services Tax (GST) Act, 2017
- GST registration and compliance
- Timely filing of returns
- Proper invoicing and documentation

## 2. Cryptocurrency Regulations

### 2.1 Legal Status
- Blockchain technology is legal in India
- Cryptocurrency trading is permitted on compliant exchanges
- NivixPe operates as a payment service provider, not as an exchange

### 2.2 Compliance Measures
- No anonymous transactions
- Transaction monitoring for suspicious activities
- Compliance with tax regulations for virtual digital assets
- Adherence to RBI guidelines for payment systems
